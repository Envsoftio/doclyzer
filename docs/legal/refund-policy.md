## 1. Subscription Plans (Pro, Family, etc.)

### 1.1 Free trial (if offered)

- If we offer a free trial, you may cancel before the trial ends and not be charged. No refund is needed because no payment was taken.

### 1.2 Paid subscription periods

- **Refund window:** You may request a full refund within **14 days** of the initial charge for a new subscription (first payment only). Refunds for renewal payments are not offered unless required by law (e.g. in certain EU countries) or at our discretion in exceptional cases.
- **How to request:** Contact us at [support@doclyzer.com or your support email] with your account email and the transaction date. We will process eligible refunds to the original payment method within **5–10 business days** (timing may depend on your bank or payment provider).
- **After refund:** Your access to paid features will end when the refund is processed. You may continue to use the Service on the free tier if your account remains active.

### 1.3 Cancellation (no refund for unused time)

- You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future charges. We do **not** refund the remaining portion of the current billing period unless you are within the 14-day refund window above or we are required by law to do so.

## 2. One-Time Purchases (e.g. Report Packs)

- **Eligibility:** You may request a full refund within **14 days** of purchase if the product was not delivered, not as described, or otherwise defective.
- **How to request:** Contact us at [support@doclyzer.com] with your account email, order/transaction details, and reason. We will review and, if eligible, process the refund to the original payment method within **5–10 business days**.
- **Non-refundable after use:** Once you have used the one-time purchase (e.g. generated or downloaded the report pack), we generally do not offer refunds unless there was a technical failure on our side.

- If you subscribed or purchased through the **Apple App Store** or **Google Play**, payment and refunds are handled by Apple or Google. Please use the refund and subscription management options in your device’s store settings (e.g. Apple: Settings → Subscriptions; Google: Play Store → Subscriptions). We cannot issue refunds for store purchases directly; we can only support you with account or access issues.

- Subscription fees after the 14-day refund window has passed.
- Renewal payments for subscription periods you have already used.
- One-time purchases that have been fully used and were delivered as described.
- Refunds requested for change of mind after extended use, except where we choose to make an exception.
