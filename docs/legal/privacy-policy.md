## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information you provide

- **Account data:** Email address, name (if you provide it), and password or other sign-in credentials.
- **Profile data:** Names and details of patient profiles you create (e.g. "Me", "Mom")—you choose what to enter.
- **Report data:** Medical reports and documents you upload (e.g. PDFs). This may include health information such as lab results, imaging reports, and other medical content. We process this only to provide the Service (e.g. parsing, summaries, charts, share links).
- **Communications:** Messages you send to us (e.g. support requests, feedback).

### 1.2 Information collected automatically

- **Usage and analytics:** We collect anonymised or aggregate data about how you use the Service—e.g. screen views, actions such as "report uploaded" or "share link created"—to improve the product and understand usage. We do **not** include your report content or personal health information in analytics.
- **Device and app data:** Device type, OS version, app version, and crash/error reports (e.g. via Crashlytics) to fix bugs and improve stability. We avoid sending any personally identifiable health data in crash reports.
- **Landing site:** If you visit our marketing/landing website, we may collect standard web data (e.g. IP address, browser type, pages visited) and use cookies or similar technologies as described in the "Cookies and similar technologies" section.

### 1.3 Information we do not sell

We do **not** sell your personal information or your health data. We do not use your report content or health information for advertising targeting.
